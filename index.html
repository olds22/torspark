About Torspark

Besides, this means that you're always communicating with the person who will actually be doing the work. The Torspark Process

30 minute free consultation

I'll start with a free 30 minute phone call to get a quick understanding of your business and see how I can help. This is where I'll ask you about your project to make sure it's a good fit before we proceed any further.

Trial Project

If your schedule allows, I like to start with a smaller related project before committing to the whole thing. This gives us both a chance to "kick the tires" and ensure that our work styles are compatible. I usually bill the Trial Project at a discount rate to account for the time it takes to ramp up. This also gives us a chance to build a strong foundation for our working relationship.

Agile + Kanban + Chaos Model of Development

I've experimented with a variety of consulting and software development methodologies, and I've come to favor a blend of a few diffent styles. I like to work closely with the client/product owner/business person (you) via Agile Software Development methods, while using a Kanban system to manage and track our day to day progress, in which we use the guiding principles of the Chaos Model to prioritize our tasks.

That's really just a fancy way of saying that I want to work with you in an iterative and adaptive way, while ensuring the important stuff that impacts your bottom line gets done first.

Full Project

After the Trial Project is complete, We should have a good feel for how well we can work together on a larger scale. With this knowledge in hand, it'll be easy to talk some numbers and figure out a solid plan of attack that will lead to a significant ROI.
